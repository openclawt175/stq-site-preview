About
Akiva Glazerson spent over a decade at the highest levels of institutional finance — including PIMCO, Lido Advisors ($40B+ AUM), Royal Bank of Canada, UBS, and Deutsche Bank — building and optimizing multi-asset portfolios for the world's largest investors.

He founded STQ Capital Partners on a simple observation: the strategies that protect institutional wealth are rarely applied to individual investors. Most advisors charge 1% to buy three ETFs. STQ charges less to build tax-aware, options-enhanced, institutionally-constructed portfolios custom-fit to your tax return.

Akiva holds a B.S. from Yeshiva University, FINRA Series 7, 63, and 65 licenses, the CSRIC designation, and the CAIA alternatives credential. He's also a Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu black belt — because discipline on the mat translates to discipline in the portfolio.
