04 — Spacing & Clear Zone

Logo Protection Area

Maintain a minimum clear zone equal to the height of the "S" in STQ on all sides. No text, graphics, or other elements may intrude into this space.

S-height S-height S-height S-height
STQ / Capital Partners

Minimum size

The logo should never be displayed smaller than 120px wide (digital) or 30mm (print) to maintain legibility of the secondary text.

Icon mark minimum

The STQ icon mark can be used as small as 24px (favicons) but should be at least 48px for general use to maintain stroke clarity.

06 — Watermark

Background Watermark Usage

The STQ watermark adds subtle brand presence to layouts and documents. Use it as a large-scale background element at very low opacity.

The watermark uses the STQ icon mark at 3–5% opacity. It should be centered or positioned in the lower-right quadrant of the layout. Never use it where it interferes with readability.

STQ

On dark backgrounds — cream at 5% opacity

On light backgrounds — navy at 4% opacity

Watermark Specifications

Opacity: 3–5% maximum

Color: Navy on light, Cream on dark

Scale: At least 2× the content area width
